# RULES LAST UPDATED: 23 APRIL, 2026
Hello, and welcome to the GeoFS PlaneFinder series! For those of you who are new here, here is a quick overview of the game. Every week, I will post a Google Form containing a screenshot, and you have to try to guess the airport it was taken at. Each time you guess correctly, you will receive a number of points and be listed on the weekly-updated leaderboard! The '''standard''' difficulty levels and scoring systems are listed as follows:
* **0 to 3/11**: Pretty much free points if you have mild knowledge about airports or are a frequent air traveler. You will score 10 points if you get one of these right.
* **4 to 7/11**: Moderate difficulty, requires a decent knowledge of the real-world location and the in-game replica. You will score 20 points if you get one of these right.
* **8 to 11/11**: Pretty difficult, but you still have some chance if you are a true avgeek. You will get score 50 points if you get one of these right.
* **IMPOSSIBLE/11**: Cooked. Deep-fried. Boiled. And whatever that's left of your brain have a 0.01% chance of survival. You will score 100 points if you somehow make it.

Sometimes, along the description of the image, you will also notice a couple of symbols. Here is a brief explanation on what they mean.

| Symbol | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **🛬** | The airline operates here in real life, using the aircraft type shown in the screenshot.|
| **🛫** | The airline/aircraft used to fly here, but no longer does. Maybe they went bankrupt...| 
| **⛱️** | This route is only operated during a certain part of the year, aka seasonal routes. |

>[!NOTE]
>There are also a few special rules added for every new season, so be sure to also check those out!
